Cards

Display custom data like games, posts or videos with the card component what is a great way to make your website cleaner to read.

Example

Cards are built with as little markup and styles as possible, but still manage to deliver a ton of control and customization.

Card title

Do greatest at in learning steepest. Breakfast extremity suffering one who all otherwise suspected.

Read more 
<div class="card">
    <img class="card-img-top" src="..." alt="Card image cap">
    <div class="card-body">
        <h5 class="card-title">Card title</h5>
        <p class="card-text">Do greatest at in learning steepest. Breakfast extremity suffering one who all otherwise suspected.</p>
        <a href="#" class="btn btn-outline">Read more</a>
    </div>
</div>
